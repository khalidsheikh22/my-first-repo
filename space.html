Our solar system

What is the solar system?

Solar means 'of the sun'. The solar system is centred around the sun, the shining ball in the sky. It includes the family of nine planets orbiting (travelling around) the sun, and smaller objects, such as comets, asteroids, and bits of space rock. The powerful pull of an invisible force called gravity from the sun stops these bodies from flying off into depest space.
